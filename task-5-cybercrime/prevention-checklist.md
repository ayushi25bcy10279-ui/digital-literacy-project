Stay Safe Online Checklist
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Always verify the sender before sharing information
Use strong and unique passwords
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Never share OTPs or banking details with anyone
Use official apps and websites for transactions
Regularly update your passwords
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
